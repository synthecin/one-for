Angelique was born in Nassau, the Bahamas on February 7, 1998, and raised in a family with a broad musical background. Her music producer father, Gregg White, was the first to sign Arrested Development, an alternative hip-hop group. Angelique has grown up as a music student. As a teenager, she began studying piano, guitar, and drums and spent extensive personal time studying Broadway and Disney music, as well as artists such as Frank Sinatra and Diana Ross.
